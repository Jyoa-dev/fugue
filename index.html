What is fugue?

fugue creates ephemeral encrypted rooms — no accounts, no servers storing your data, no history.

Type a room name, share it with someone, and chat. Close the tab and everything vanishes: messages, files, identities. Nothing is retained.

End-to-end encryption is enabled when you set a shared passphrase. The relay only sees ciphertext.
